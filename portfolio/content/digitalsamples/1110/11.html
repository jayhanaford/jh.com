The software includes a very simple loop whose duration is based on the 33/45 switch. The duration of the loop, basically 100µs for 33T speed, is modified in relation to the position of the rotary switch. During these 100µs, the microprocessor incrementally reads the value of the signal to be generated in a look-up table.

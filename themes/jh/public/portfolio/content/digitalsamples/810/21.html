While bad room acoustics are often to blame for a less than optimal listening experience, the solutions are often sought in new equipment, cables, or recordings. One of the companies offering a wide range of products to solve acoustic problems is Vicoustic (www.vicoustic.com). From bass traps and absorption panels to diffusion panels and accessories, they have it. So next time you are not satisfied with the overall audio quality of your audio system, you might consider experimenting with the room acoustics.

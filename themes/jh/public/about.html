I am a Graphic Designer based in Boston, MA. specializing in web design, print design, corporate identity and branding. I create clean, creative, and functional designs. On this website you will find my personal graphic design portfolio and my contact info (my design services are currently available for hire). I am very passionate, organized, and focused with my work. I also love many forms of art and have several hobbies including backpacking, photography, and kayaking.

I am a perfectionist, but I always consider time as an important factor. My experience has given me the ability to easily adapt from being a designer, to coder, to decision maker. My adaptability shows that I can handle any task I’m given whether it be print, branding, multimedia, or web.

I design with Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, and Flash. I code sites with XHTML and CSS.

I have over six years of experience as the graphic director for a publisher. During this time, I designed concepts, identity, execution, and presentation of numerous projects: a monthly subscription-based magazine, two business-to-business periodicals, website design, books, interface design, direct-mail packages, magazine advertisements, web advertisements, product catalogues, and company branding.


Other Highlights:
Designed and developed the company website which includes e-commerce and other interactive material.

Created Flash-based digital editions of our magazines for use on the PC, Mac, iPad, and the Android OS.

Saved thousands of dollars per year in production costs by implementing alternative production methods.
